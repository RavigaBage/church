• Zoe Missions Control

    This is a platforms is created to give limited access to out-research departments and ministries to collect and record data, as such any individual without the proper clearance will not be given access to this sites content.To get Clearance contact the administrator, of the Zoe worship centre at kasoa

  • Data record Information

    The Zoe Mission control platform contains Groups in which appointed individuals will belong to, each individual after a successful login will be assigned to a specific group. As such any record recorded by the user will be label to such group

  • Information Sharing

    Users of this platform will be notified of any information regarding evangelism,outreach etc to be performed by by the church, note that such information will be shared by the administrator

  • User Autonomy

    No user will not be forced to join missions he/she does not want to partake in. Any discomfort or situational change which might occur must be communicated to the administrator prior the agreed date of a mission.
